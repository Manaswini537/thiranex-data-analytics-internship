Superstore Sales & Revenue Dashboard


 📌 Objective
To analyze retail sales data and generate meaningful business insights using Power BI.

 🛠 Tools Used
- Power BI
- Superstore Dataset (Excel)

 📊 Key Features
- KPI Cards (Total Sales, Total Profit, Total Orders)
- Sales Trend Analysis (Line Chart)
- Top Products Analysis
- Region-wise Sales Distribution
- Category-wise Performance
- Interactive Filters (Slicers)


 🔍 Key Insights
-West region has the highest sale
-Sales initially started low, experienced a decline, and then showed a steady increase over the years.
-Technology category generates the highest revenue
-The Canon imageCLASS 2200 Advanced Copier is one of the top-performing products, contributing significantly to overall sales
-Some products generate high sales but relatively low profit, indicating the impact of discounts or higher costs.
